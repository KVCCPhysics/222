Weekly Tasks (14 Weeks)

(0%) Lecture (In-Person, two per week)

  • Attendance not recorded
  • Phy222 is hard. I hope to explain the hard parts in class
  • Use the book as another helpful teacher. The example problems are a great resource.

(10%) Quiz 1-14 (on Canvas, one per week)

  • 14 Quizzes over 14 weeks (one per week)
  • Infinite attempts, try to test your understanding
  • Keep up with lecture and make sure you are grasping the key concepts
  • Complete these in tandem with the homework sets

(10%) Homework 1-14 (on paper, one set per week)

  • 14 Homework sets over 14 weeks (one per week)
  • Used as practice. You must work problems to learn the skill.
  • Must show your work to get credit. Checkout with me in person or over email.
  • These are important problems to know. solutions provided.
  • Exam questions are adapted from these homework questions.

(20%) Lab (In-Person, one per week)

  • 10 Labs over 14 weeks (skipping exam weeks)
  • Must attend for credit

(60%) In Class Exam (approx one per month)

  • Four in-class paper exams.
  • This is where you earn a bunch of your final grade.
  • Questions will be adapted from problems in the unit homework set.
  • You may postpone taking exams by up to two weeks, but try not to.

Approximate Exam Dates:

  • Exam Unit 1: Feb 5th
  • Exam Unit 2: Feb 26th
  • Exam Unit 3: April 2nd
  • Exam Unit 4: April 23th
